# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Designers and marketers who have a local HTML design (landing / H5 long page) and need editable Figma layers without a Figma plugin. They work on a desktop browser, often with Chinese content, and want a short path: open file → preview → copy → paste.

## Product Purpose

web-to-figma turns a local HTML document (plus optional asset folder) into a Figma clipboard payload. Success is: the on-screen preview matches the design closely enough to trust, and one click yields pasteable layers in Figma. Hosting may be Docker; conversion always runs in the browser.

## Brand Personality

Focused, quiet, instrumental — a utility that disappears into the task.

## Anti-references

- Marketing landing pages with long feature explanations
- Card-heavy dashboards and multi-column wizards for a three-action flow
- Decorative glassmorphism, giant hero type, purple gradient defaults

## Design Principles

1. Preview is the product — chrome stays out of the way.
2. One row of controls, zero tutorials on the main surface.
3. Status only when it changes decisions (missing assets, errors).
4. Familiar tool affordances (top bar + canvas), not a marketing site.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Keyboard-reachable file pickers and primary action
- Visible focus rings on toolbar controls
- Prefer reduced motion (no load choreography)
- WCAG AA contrast on toolbar text and buttons
